THE DREAMLINKERS

Sometimes, dreams do come true.
And sometimes, somebody needs to stop them.

Celine and Adam are two British teenagers from troubled backgrounds, who are thrust together when they discover that they have the ability to communicate with each other in their dreams.

Through a mysterious organisation called The Sleep Agency, the pair discover that their dreams come with a unique extra power: they're sometimes able to predict future events.

As Celine and Adam work to understand their powers, can The Sleep Agency help the pair to discover their full potential? And, between them, can they prevent an unthinkable tragedy from occurring?
