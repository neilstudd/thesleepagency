THE DREAMLINKERS

Sometimes, dreams do come true.
And sometimes, somebody needs to stop them.

Celine and Adam are two British teenagers from troubled backgrounds, who are thrust together when they discover that they have the ability to communicate with each other in their dreams.

Through a mysterious organisation called The Sleep Agency, the pair discover that their dreams come with a unique extra power: they're sometimes able to predict future events.

As Celine and Adam work to understand their powers, can The Sleep Agency help the pair to discover their full potential? And, between them, can they prevent an unthinkable tragedy from occurring?

THE NIGHTMARE PROSPECT

The dreamlinkers live in secret.
Their powers are growing.
And now, someone’s out to kill them.

The Sleep Agency, and its finest recruits, are reeling in the face of a global catastrophe. But as they seek to perform damage control, it appears as if dark forces are starting to hunt them down in their dreams.

Meanwhile, in China, an aspiring military official is trying to piece together the mysteries of dreamlinking for herself, but what will her bosses do with the information that they discover?

In a globe-spanning adventure, can Adam and Celine help to unravel a conspiracy which threatens to bring the world to the brink of war - and can they avoid being marked for death themselves?
