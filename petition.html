Petition Sign a Dreamlinker's Charter into law

We call upon the government to institute a charter to regulate the usage of 'dreamlinking' powers and protect British citizens against attack.

More details

The British people deserve protection from individuals with dreamlinking powers - both those from within the UK, and from abroad.

The Daily Mail and Mail on Sunday have proposed a starting point for such a charter, which would guard the nation's citizens against being targeted while they are sleeping in their beds.

We believe that a lack of actions so far represents culpability for the government in the deaths of numerous citizens, and we call on the government to take steps to prevent future attacks.
